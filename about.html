How it all started ?

Addmix Factory...
New technology for manufacturing premixes in Egypt. Our main goal is to give the breeder high quality formulas to raise the efficiency of the animal.

Addmix is a part of the Egyptian Arab Group for Veterinary Services (EGAVET), which is a leading company in the field of veterinary services All products related to animal, poultry and fish production in Egypt by providing the latest services in the field of Biosecurity.

EGAVET was established in 1992 and is considered one of the leading and most trusted companies for products and services of international quality during more than 30 years in the Egyptian market.

our values

We have been established with the aim of supporting animal production by producing safe, high-quality fodder that guarantees the best value Economical and highest conversion factor.

Goals

There are many goals and challenges that the Admix factory seeks to achieve, including “covering and supplying Egypt and the East.” Also the middle ground with products that are safe for human health in the first place and animal health - the Admix factory targets the manufacture of feed additives for pets of high quality for the availability of an integrated healthy ecosystem for human health.

Quality Control

The Admix factory manufactures feed additives with the necessity of having accurate internal analysis capabilities for all vitamins and minerals, as no raw materials are accepted without conforming to quality standards or without verifying all production requirements, thus we ensure that each customer obtains his requirements in the product specifications.

Our lab

The factory has a laboratory equipped with the latest equipment in the global market, which is managed by a team of experts and specialists who perform periodic tests to ensure the highest levels of quality, starting from receiving the raw materials until reaching the final product.

Distribution

The Admix factory covers all local governorates throughout the Arab Republic of Egypt, as well The local scope such as "Libya - Yemen - Iraq" and all countries of the Middle East.
